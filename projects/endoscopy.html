Medical Endoscopy Simulation with Hardware-in-the-Loop RL

At the Experimental Soft Robotics Lab (ESL), I worked on developing a simulation framework for robotic endoscopy. The goal was to improve fiber-scanning patterns while accounting for physical constraints like photobleaching.

Contributions

  • Modeled fiber-scanning endoscopy system dynamics in simulation.
  • Implemented hardware-in-the-loop RL to optimize scanning policies.
  • Tested methods to minimize photobleaching while maintaining image quality.
  • Collaborated with lab members on integrating real hardware with the simulation loop.

Outcomes

  • Pipeline established for connecting RL controllers to real endoscopy prototypes.
  • Promising early results on improved scan efficiency and reduced photobleaching effects.
